Our Business


Layer Poultry Farms

We work closely with layer poultry farmers through our integrated farm model to ensure quality at every step—from feed to farming to final delivery—so you get eggs that are fresh, hygienic, and truly nutritious. Our hens are nourished with carefully formulated, high-quality feed enriched with essential nutrients, vitamins, and minerals, resulting in stronger shells, better yolk quality, and higher nutritional value in every egg.
We provide scientifically balanced, nutrient-rich feed to support optimal growth, strong immunity, and consistent egg production, with every batch carefully sourced and tested for safety and quality. Cleanliness is a top priority across all our farms, with strict hygiene protocols in housing and egg handling to prevent contamination and ensure freshness. Our birds are under constant veterinary supervision, including timely vaccinations and health checks to maintain flock health.
Eggs are collected, sorted, and stored through hygienic processes to minimize breakage and preserve quality. With complete traceability from feed to farm to final packaging, we ensure full transparency and trust, while our integrated system eliminates middlemen—helping farmers get fair value and customers receive high-quality eggs at the best price.

Cage-Free Poultry

We work in close integration with farmers who operate cage-free poultry setups, building a strong network focused on quality, hygiene, and ethical farming. We believe that better farming creates better food, and our cage-free poultry integration system is designed to deliver ultra-clean, safe, and high-quality eggs while upholding the highest standards of animal welfare and farm hygiene.
Unlike traditional poultry farming, this cage-free integration allows hens to live in a stress-free, open environment where they can move naturally, perch comfortably, and lay eggs in clean, designated nest areas. Every aspect of the farm is carefully engineered to ensure purity, hygiene, and consistency.
This approach reflects a modern transformation in egg production—focused on animal welfare, productivity, and sustainability—where hens are not confined to cages but raised in well-managed open barns or aviaries. These systems seamlessly integrate feeding, watering, egg collection, and waste management to ensure both efficiency and bird comfort. By allowing hens to express natural behaviors, cage-free systems reduce stress and improve overall health, resulting in eggs that are cleaner, stronger in shell quality, and produced in a more ethical and sustainable way.


Free-Range Poultry

We work in close integration with farmers who raise free-range birds, creating a reliable network dedicated to producing high-quality eggs. Free-range poultry farming is a natural and ethical approach to egg production where hens are given access to open outdoor spaces along with well-managed indoor housing. This system allows birds to roam freely, forage for natural food, and express instinctive behaviors such as pecking, scratching, and dust bathing, which reduces stress and improves overall health. By combining controlled farm management with outdoor freedom, free-range systems ensure better living conditions while maintaining hygiene and productivity. Eggs produced in free-range environments are often valued for their freshness, strong shell quality, and rich nutritional profile. This method also supports sustainable farming practices by promoting better waste distribution and a balanced ecosystem, reflecting a commitment to animal welfare, environmental responsibility, and the production of clean, naturally sourced eggs.
