OUR STORY

Empowering Farmers with Digital Strength, Bringing Fresh Produce Closer to You

With 15+ years of experience in the IT industry, I bring deep expertise in building digital solutions and scalable systems. Today, I am channeling that experience into agriculture — with a clear mission to empower farmers. By leveraging technology and digital marketing, I aim to connect farmers directly with customers, eliminating middlemen and increasing their profitability.


This initiative is about creating a transparent, efficient, and sustainable ecosystem where farmers earn more, and customers receive fresh, trusted produce directly from the source. Bridging technology and agriculture for a better future. 🌱

Director
Dr. Ganesh Wagh
NovelnestAgro Pvt Ltd

Transforming Farmers’ Lives Through Innovation, Knowledge, and Direct Market Access

Dr. Ganesh Gopinath Wagh is a dedicated farmer with over 15 years of experience in the field of agriculture. On January 25, 2025, he was honored at the “Bharat-Nirman Contribution Award 2025” event held in Mumbai. At this event, Dr. Wagh from Nashik district received an honorary doctorate from an American university for his excellent work in agriculture. The program was organized by the Empower Social and Education Trust and recognized many talented people from different fields. Dr. Wagh was specially appreciated for his work in farming and flower cultivation, especially for his expertise in growing flowers in polyhouses.


Dr. Wagh envisions empowering fellow farmers by guiding and educating them in the cultivation of various agricultural products. His mission is to help farmers achieve success by integrating them with a structured system that improves their direct connectivity with consumers. Through this initiative, farmers can benefit from better market access, fair pricing, and increased profitability.
