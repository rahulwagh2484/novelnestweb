Hygiegs Premium Egg Packaging

Freshness, Nutrition & Hygiene in Every Pack

At Hygiegs, we go beyond packaging—we deliver nutrition, safety, and trust in every egg. Our eggs are carefully collected, cleaned, and packed under hygienic conditions to preserve their natural freshness and quality from farm to table.
Each Hygiegs pack is designed to protect eggs from damage while maintaining their internal quality. A fresh egg naturally consists of three parts—the shell, egg white, and yolk, each playing a vital role in nutrition. The egg white forms about 60% of the egg and is rich in high-quality protein, while the yolk contains essential fats, vitamins, and minerals needed for overall health.

What truly sets our eggs apart is our “Pro-Yolk Gold” advantage—rich, vibrant yolks packed with superior nutrition, enhanced antioxidants, and essential nutrients that support better health, energy, and overall wellness

Eggs are considered one of the most nutrient-dense foods, providing:
Rich in anti-oxidant pigment for superior anti-aging power
High-quality protein for muscle strength and growth
Healthy fats and energy for daily performance
Essential vitamins (A, D, E, B-complex) for immunity and wellness
Important minerals like calcium, iron, and phosphorus for strong bones and blood health

With approximately 173 kcal energy and 13.3g protein per 100g, eggs are a smart and balanced choice for a healthy lifestyle.
Beyond nutrition, eggs are naturally versatile and functional—they act as binding, foaming, and emulsifying agents, making them an essential part of everyday cooking and food products.

At Hygiegs, we ensure:
Clean and hygienic packaging standards
Careful handling to maintain freshness and shelf life
Proper labeling for transparency and consumer trust
Storage guidance to retain quality and nutrition

Our eggs are packed to minimize quality loss, as freshness directly affects egg quality—over time, natural changes like moisture loss and air cell expansion can occur. That is why we emphasize proper storage and timely consumption.
Whether you choose a 6, 12, 18, or 30-pack, Hygiegs ensures that every egg delivers consistent quality, superior nutrition, and farm-fresh goodness.
