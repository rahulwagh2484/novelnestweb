Polyhouse Flower Farming

We specialize in high-value chrysanthemum cultivation under advanced polyhouse systems, ensuring consistent quality, vibrant colors, and longer vase life. Chrysanthemum, popularly known as Shevanti, is one of the most demanded flowers in domestic and export markets.
Our polyhouse farming technique creates a controlled microclimate where temperature, humidity, and light are optimized for maximum flower yield. This results in uniform blooming, superior stem strength, and premium-grade flowers ideal for bouquets, decorations, and events.

Key Highlights:

  • 🌼 Premium white, yellow, green & exotic chrysanthemum varieties
  • 🌿 Uniform flowering with high stem quality
  • 💧 Drip irrigation & fertigation for efficient nutrient use
  • 🌡 Climate-controlled polyhouse for year-round production
  • 📦 Better shelf life & transport-friendly flowers

Premium Chrysanthemum Farming

Premium chrysanthemum farming is an advanced cultivation approach focused on producing high-quality, uniform flowers with large blooms, vibrant colors, and strong, long stems. These flowers are typically grown in controlled environments such as polyhouses or greenhouses, where temperature, humidity, and light can be precisely managed to ensure consistent quality throughout the year.>

Since chrysanthemum is a short-day plant, farmers carefully regulate light exposure to control flowering cycles and align production with high-demand periods like weddings, festivals, and export seasons. Modern cultivation techniques such as pinching, disbudding, drip irrigation, and precise fertigation play a crucial role in enhancing flower size, stem strength, and overall yield.

Additionally, effective pest and disease management, along with proper post-harvest handling, helps maintain freshness and extend shelf life. With strong demand in decoration, bouquets, and international markets, premium chrysanthemum farming offers high profitability. However, it also requires significant investment, technical knowledge, and careful management to achieve consistent success.

Key Highlights:

  • 🌼 Grown in polyhouse/greenhouse for premium quality
  • ✂️ Practices like pinching & disbudding improve flower size
  • 💧 Uses drip irrigation & fertigation for better growth
  • 🌞 Light control helps manage flowering time
  • 🐛 Requires proper pest & disease management
  • 📦 Careful post-harvest handling increases shelf life
  • 💰 High demand in bouquets, decoration & export markets
