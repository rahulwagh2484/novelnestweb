Who We Are

NovelnestAgro Private Limited is a forward-thinking agricultural company focused on blending modern technology with traditional farming practices. Our mission is to empower farmers by providing direct market access, eliminating middlemen, and ensuring better profitability.

We specialize in Poultry Integration Farming, hygienic egg production, polyhouse flower cultivation, and agricultural training. With a strong digital backbone, we bridge the gap between farmers and consumers.
